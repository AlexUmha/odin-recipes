Duch's Godlike Brownies

Duch's Godlike Brownies are the undeniable pinnacle of brownie perfection. There exists no alternative, no competitor, and certainly no usurper capable of stealing their throne of superiority. Duncan Hines? You're joking, right? Betty Crocker? Well, aren't we a bundle of laughs today? And I like Ghiradelli as much as the next guy, but come on now, they'll have to step aside, because even they could not hope to hold a candle to these beauts.

Ingredients

  • An 8x8 baking pan
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3⁄4 cup sugar
  • 4 oz baking chocolate, unsweetened
  • 8 oz chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 2 eggs
  • 3⁄4 cup flour
  • 1⁄4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp water

Directions

  1. Combine butter and sugar
  2. Bring mix to a boil, then remove from range
  3. Add chocolate and chocolate chips; stir thoroughly
  4. Add vanilla
  5. Beat in eggs, one at a time
  6. Blend in flour and baking soda
  7. Add water and mix until consistent
  8. Finally, bake at 325° for 30 minutes
